Life Balancing

Find practical advice on how to manage daily responsibilities while making time for yourself. From setting boundaries to creating healthy routines, explore tips that help you maintain balance between work, school, and personal life.

  • Wake up at the same time every day
  • Limit screen time before bed to improve sleep quality.
  • Take short walks daily to clear your mind and reduce stress.
  • Say no to extra tasks when your schedule is already full.
  • Eat meals on time to maintain energy and focus.
